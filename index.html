A Louisville Tradition Since 1933

Jack Fry's has been a cornerstone of Louisville's dining scene for over nine decades. What began as a sportsman's hangout has evolved into a fine dining institution, blending Southern hospitality with exceptional cuisine.

Our historic walls, adorned with vintage photographs, tell the story of our rich heritage while our kitchen creates contemporary Southern and American bistro fare with French influences.
