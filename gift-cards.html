Frequently Asked Questions

Do gift cards expire?

No, Jack Fry's gift cards never expire. They can be used anytime for dining in our restaurant.

Can I use a gift card for online orders?

Currently, gift cards can only be redeemed for in-restaurant dining. They cannot be used for takeout or delivery.

What if I lose my gift card?

Please keep your gift card in a safe place. Lost or stolen cards cannot be replaced unless you have the card number and purchase receipt.

Can I check my gift card balance?

Yes, you can check your balance by calling us at (502) 452-9244 during restaurant hours.

Are there any fees?

No, there are no activation fees, maintenance fees, or expiration fees for our gift cards.

Can I use multiple gift cards?

Yes, you can use multiple gift cards for a single transaction. Any remaining balance will stay on the card for future use.
