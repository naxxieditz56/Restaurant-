Accessibility & Guest Information

Committed to welcoming all guests with comfort and care

Welcome to Jack Fry's

At Jack Fry's, we believe that exceptional dining experiences should be accessible to everyone. Our historic building has been thoughtfully updated to accommodate guests with diverse needs while preserving its 1930s charm.

We encourage guests with specific accessibility requirements to contact us in advance so we can ensure your visit is comfortable and enjoyable.

Accessibility Features

Mobility Access

  • Designated accessible parking space
  • Ramp entrance (no steps)
  • Wide hallways and aisles
  • Accessible restrooms
  • Wheelchair-friendly tables available

Visual Assistance

  • Staff trained to assist visually impaired guests
  • Reading glasses available on request
  • Flashlight or magnifying glass available
  • Large print menu available with advance notice
  • Well-lit dining areas

Hearing Assistance

  • Quieter table placement available
  • Staff trained in clear communication
  • Written communication accommodated
  • Advance notice for optimal seating

Health & Dietary

  • Detailed allergy information available
  • Staff trained in food allergy protocols
  • Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options
  • Advance notice for complex dietary needs

Parking & Entrance

Accessible Parking: One reserved space located closest to the entrance with an access aisle. Additional street parking available.

Entrance: Ramp access with no steps. Door width: 36 inches. Automatic door opener available upon request.

Pathway: Smooth, level surface from parking to entrance.

Interior Navigation

Hallways: Minimum width of 42 inches throughout restaurant.

Tables: Several tables can accommodate wheelchairs (please request when booking).

Mobility Device Storage: Can be stored in locked office or kept near your table.

Lighting: Ambient lighting with adjustable brightness in certain areas.

Restrooms

Accessibility: Each restroom has a wheelchair-accessible stall.

Men's Room: Accessible urinal and stall with grab bars.

Women's Room: Accessible stall with grab bars.

Gender Accessibility: Trans and gender-nonconforming guests are welcome to use the restroom that feels most comfortable.

Service Animals

Service animals are always welcome at Jack Fry's. Please inform us when making your reservation so we can ensure appropriate seating.

Water bowls available upon request.

Please arrive at your reservation time. Tables are held for 15 minutes past reservation time.

Photography

Personal photography is welcome. Professional photography requires advance approval.

Children

Children are welcome. We offer a limited children's menu and high chairs are available.

Emergency Information

Our staff is trained in basic first aid and emergency response. In case of emergency:

  • Notify any staff member immediately
  • First aid kit available
  • Defibrillator (AED) on premises
  • Emergency exits clearly marked
